ABOUT

   WHAT IS ARMS DETECTION

Arms detection is the identification and recognition of firearms.


Our arms detection AI is utilizing the TensorFlow Model Zoo's SSD MobileNet V2 FPNLite 320x320 enableing real-time identification of concealed weapons, revolutionizing security protocols and preventing potential threats. This efficient and accurate technology enhances public safety by swiftly detecting firearms, empowering security teams to respond promptly and mitigate risks. The lightweight architecture of SSD MobileNet V2 FPNLite ensures rapid processing, making it an effective tool for arms detection in various high-security environments.
